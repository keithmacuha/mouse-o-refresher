Our Story

A cultural haven that transcends time and space. Our story begins with a passion for preserving and showcasing the rich tapestry of human history and creativity. Mouseío has evolved into a dynamic institution committed to inspiring curiosity, fostering learning, and celebrating the beauty of our shared heritage.

Our Mission

Mouseío is dedicated to curating and presenting diverse exhibitions, collections, and educational programs that engage, educate, and entertain. Our mission is to be a hub for intellectual and artistic exploration, promoting inclusivity and fostering a sense of wonder. Through collaboration with local and global communities, we aim to enrich lives by making culture accessible to all.

Our Vision

At Mouseío, our vision is to be a beacon of knowledge, creativity, and cultural exchange. We aspire to ignite the spark of curiosity in every visitor, encouraging a deeper understanding of the past and present, and inspiring a brighter future through the lens of art, history, and innovation.

Our Purpose

The purpose of Mouseío is to serve as a bridge between generations, cultures, and ideas. By preserving and sharing the stories that define us, we contribute to the collective wisdom of humanity. Mouseío is not just a space for artifacts; it's a dynamic platform that encourages dialogue, stimulates creativity, and nurtures a love for lifelong learning.
